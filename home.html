Science and Technology panorama of the European region called Western Balkans

The Western Balkans region involves several countries, each of them with different economies, innovation policies and distinct scientific/technological production.

Context:


Countries:

  • Albania - Bosnia and Herzegovina - Kosovo - Macedonia - Montenegro - Serbia

Research programmes:

  • CIP: Competitiveness and Innovation Framework Programme
  • Creative Europe: Supporting Europe's cultural and creative sectors
  • Culture: Education, Audiovisual and Culture
  • Eureka: International R&D&I cooperation
  • FP7:Framework Programme for Research and Technological Development
  • H2020: EU's biggest Framework Programme for Research and Innovation

Objetive:


  • Measure the potential for scientific collaboration between different actors
  • Interactive information about tendencies, trends and leaders in each sector/activity

Aim:

What does these interactive visualizations address:

  • • How do the existing organizations collaborate together? how is this collaboration? there are main actors? which are the groups of organizations that tend to collaborate together? How the collaboration changes through the different domains, areas, etc..?
  • • How has the R&I activity evolved over the years? which are the trends for the existing domains, intervention areas or types of activity?
  • • Which are the main, most productive actors in Western Balkans? Which is its contribution relative to the overall R&I production?

Visualization

This interactive visualization tool provides a user friendly interface, which combines overview of the Research & Innovation activities produced by these countries and enable exploration of the level of collaboration between them .

Information about R&D projects across different EU programmes and its relationship with the centers involved/affiliations are supported using different filters for selecting actors/timelines/project characteristics as needed.

About

About the data:


The following datasets describe the Research&Innovation activity of the Western Balkans region, as well as information about the actors (universities, private companies, research centers, etc) participating in these R&D activities.

Activities:

  • • riactivityId: Unique identifier for a given activity
  • • riactivityTypeName: Descriptor of the activity. All values different from 'Patent' are considered R&I projects related to the different funding programmes promoted by the Europan Commision.
  • • year: Starting year of the project
  • • date: Starting date of the project
  • • domainName: how this activity is classified based on a list of possible domains
  • • interventionAreaName: how this activity is classified based on its area of intervention

    • Participations:

  • • riactivityId: Unique identifier for a given activity
  • • affiliationName: Name of the organization participating in the activity
  • • countryName: country of the organization
  • • riactivityType: Descriptor of the activity. All values different from 'Patent' are considered R&I projects related to the different funding programmes promoted by the Europan Commision.

    • Source of dataset: https://cordis.europa.eu/projects/es
